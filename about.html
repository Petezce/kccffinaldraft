Who We Are

Kiambu County Clergy Forum ia non-governmental, non-partisan and non profit making organisation upholding biblically based Christian doctrine and principles

Our Passion

Following the footsteps of the Lord Jesus Christ, and standing on His righteousness, become and grow a movement that will take a stand in ensuring that Kiambu County becomes the county that God intended her to be for her people, for the nation of Kenya and the world to the glory and honor of His name

Our Mission

To reposition the church appropriately in order to play her rightful role in; Social,Spiritual ,Economical and Political development for the welfare of the people of the county and country at large..
