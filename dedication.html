On this date 10th May 2011 through God Bless Kenya Prayer movement by the church. government and county leaders we dedicate Kiambu County for God.

A Prayer for the kiambu county

The God of our County, give us the grace and humility as the leaders of the county to confess our sins and seek your forgiveness for all the ills that have befallen our county.

May righteousness and justice be our guide in all our deliberations. Lord unite us despite our differences

that blessings and prosperity be established in our county .

in Jesus name" Amen.
