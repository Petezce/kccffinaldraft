Topic 1: Introduction

The main objective of the organisation is to strive towards good governance in the country and participate in the moral, spriritual and material progresss of the county and the common good of all Kenyans regardless of their; religion, thereby leaving a lasting transformation in the lives of beneficiaries

Topic 2: Main objectives

-To facilitate the unity of all christians chuches in the county
-To play a prophetic role and be a voice in the governance and development
-To mobilize clergy for regular fellowship and prayers for the church and the county. -To propagate christian morals and values especially amoung the youth in the county.
-To facilitate healing and reconciliation among the various groups especially the under privileged and physically challenged in the county.
-To contribute to the national church leadership and the national agenda through the national forums in the county.
-To facilitate civil education for awareness raising on the county affairs and the role of the church in societ.
-To mobilize resources and outside the county for the welfare those below the poverty line.
-To facilitate capacity building for the clergy and the church as a whole in relevant areas.
-To facilitate appropriate research for the church and society in county and establish a resource Centre/library for the youth.
